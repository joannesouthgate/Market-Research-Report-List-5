<p><p>The fused silica microlens array market encompasses two main types: aspherical and spherical microlens arrays. Aspherical microlens arrays feature non-spherical profiles, allowing for improved optical performance by minimizing aberrations and enhancing light collection efficiency. In contrast, spherical microlens arrays maintain a uniform curvature, offering simplicity and ease of manufacture. Both types find applications in various fields, including telecommunications, imaging systems, and sensors, driven by the demand for advanced optical solutions in compact designs and high-performance devices.</p></p>
