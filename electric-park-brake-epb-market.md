<p><strong>The Electric Park Brake (EPB) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cable Puller Type</li><li>Caliper Integrated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Park Brake (EPB) market consists of two primary types: Cable Puller Type and Caliper Integrated Type. The Cable Puller Type utilizes electric motors to pull cables that engage the parking brake, offering a simple installation and retrofit options. In contrast, the Caliper Integrated Type features an electric motor integrated directly within the brake caliper, enabling compact design and improved performance. Both types enhance safety, convenience, and space efficiency in modern vehicles, catering to evolving automotive technology demands.</p></p>
