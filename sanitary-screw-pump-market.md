<p><p>The sanitary screw pump market comprises two primary types: vertical screw pumps and horizontal screw pumps. Vertical screw pumps are designed for space-saving applications and facilitate easy maintenance while managing high-viscosity fluids. They are often used in food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and chemical industries. Horizontal screw pumps, on the other hand, are ideal for transferring larger volumes of liquid and are favored for their efficiency and versatility in various industrial applications.
