<p><p>Non-absorbable sutures are specialized medical devices used to close wounds or surgical incisions, made from materials that do not break down in the body over time. These sutures maintain their tensile strength and can be utilized in various surgical procedures, making them ideal for long-term tissue support. They are typically used in areas where permanent closure is necessary, such as in orthopedic, cardiovascular, and plastic surgeries.</p><p>The Non Absorbable Sutures Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.20% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing incidence of surgical procedures, advancements in suture technology, and rising demand for wound closure products in emerging markets. Additionally, there is an increasing emphasis on minimally invasive surgeries that utilize non-absorbable sutures for enhanced precision and reduced healing time.</p><p>Moreover, the introduction of innovative materials and the development of hybrid sutures that combine absorbable and non-absorbable properties are emerging trends. The growing awareness of healthcare associated with surgical site infections is also pushing the demand for high-quality non-absorbable sutures, contributing to overall market expansion.</p></p>
