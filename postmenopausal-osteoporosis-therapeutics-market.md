Raloxifene, a selective estrogen receptor modulator, provides bone protection with less breast cancer risk. Denosumab, a monoclonal antibody, targets RANKL to inhibit osteoclast activity. Other therapies may include calcium and vitamin D supplements, along with emerging medications aimed at improving bone health.</p></p>
