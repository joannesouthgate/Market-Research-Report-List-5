<p><p>The Vehicle Grade LiDAR Scanner market is primarily divided into two types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR utilizes rotating components to capture 3D data, offering high-resolution imagery but often at a higher cost and complexity. Conversely, Solid State LiDAR employs fixed configurations with no moving parts, providing a more compact, robust, and cost-effective solution, ideal for mass production in vehicles. Both types are critical for enhancing autonomous driving and advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
