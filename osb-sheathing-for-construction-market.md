<p><p>OSB sheathing is categorized into four types based on bond strength and moisture resistance. OSB/1 is suitable for dry internal use, typically in furniture. OSB/2 is designed for load-bearing applications in dry conditions. OSB/3 offers moisture resistance, making it ideal for structural applications in humid environments. OSB/4 is the strongest variant, used for high-load applications and areas exposed to moisture. Each type serves specific construction needs, ensuring structural integrity and durability in varying conditions.</p></p>
