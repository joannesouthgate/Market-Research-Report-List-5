<p><p>The venom antiserum market is categorized into two main types: polyvalent and monovalent. Polyvalent antiserum is designed to neutralize multiple types of venom, making it effective for treating bites from various snakes or other venomous creatures. In contrast, monovalent antiserum targets a specific venom type, offering heightened efficacy for singular threats. Both types cater to different clinical needs and geographic prevalence of venomous species, influencing demand in healthcare settings and wildlife conservation efforts.</p></p>
