<p><p>The polyurethane wire enamels market is divided into two main types: low heat-resistant enamel and high heat-resistant enamel. Low heat-resistant enamel is designed for applications where temperatures do not exceed moderate levels, offering sufficient insulation and flexibility. In contrast, high heat-resistant enamel can withstand elevated temperatures, making it suitable for demanding environments such as motors and transformers. These enamels provide excellent electrical insulation, mechanical protection, and thermal stability, catering to varied industrial needs and enhancing performance in specific applications.</p></p>
