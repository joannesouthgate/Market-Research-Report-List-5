<p><p>The non-isolated DC/DC converter module market primarily comprises boost and buck modules. Boost modules step up voltage levels, converting a lower input voltage to a higher output voltage, ideal for applications requiring increased power supply. Conversely, buck modules reduce voltage levels, providing a lower output voltage from a higher input voltage, making them suitable for powering devices that require reduced power levels. Both types are essential in various sectors like consumer electronics, automotive, and renewable energy systems, catering to diverse voltage regulation needs.</p></p>
