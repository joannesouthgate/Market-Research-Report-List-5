<p><p>The butyl adhesives market is primarily categorized into two types: butyl adhesive tape and butyl adhesive paste. Butyl adhesive tape is a versatile, self-adhesive solution commonly used for sealing and waterproofing applications, known for its strong adhesion and flexibility. Conversely, butyl adhesive paste is a viscous material ideal for heavy-duty bonding and repair tasks, offering excellent durability and resistance to environmental factors. Both types are widely utilized in construction, automotive, and industrial sectors for their reliability and performance.</p></p>
