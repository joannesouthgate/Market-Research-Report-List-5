<p><p>The digital pressure transmitter market comprises three main types: absolute pressure transmitters, differential pressure transmitters, and other specialized variants. Absolute pressure transmitters measure pressure relative to a complete vacuum, making them ideal for applications requiring precise atmospheric pressure readings. Differential pressure transmitters compare two pressure points, commonly used in flow measurement and filter monitoring. Other market types include gauge pressure transmitters and specialized devices tailored for specific industries, enhancing measurement accuracy and reliability across diverse applications.</p></p>
