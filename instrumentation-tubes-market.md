<p><p>The instrumentation tubes market can be categorized into two main types: seamless tubes and welded tubes. Seamless tubes are manufactured without any joints, offering superior strength and consistency, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, welded tubes are created by joining flat metal strips, making them more cost-effective and suitable for various fluid handling processes. Both types are essential in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and instrumentation, where precision and reliability are critical.</p></p>
