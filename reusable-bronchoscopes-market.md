<p><p>The reusable bronchoscopes market comprises primarily two types: rigid bronchoscopes and flexible bronchoscopes. Rigid bronchoscopes are inflexible tubes that provide a direct line of sight and are typically used in surgical settings for airway management and retrieval of foreign bodies. Flexible bronchoscopes, on the other hand, are versatile instruments with a flexible shaft, allowing for navigation through the complex anatomy of the lungs. They are commonly used for diagnostic procedures, biopsies, and therapeutic interventions, offering enhanced maneuverability and patient comfort.</p></p>
