<p><strong>The Jet Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Apron drive jet bridge</li><li>Nose-loader jet bridge</li><li>Dual jet bridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jet bridges are vital for airport operations and passenger convenience, categorized mainly into three types. The apron drive jet bridge moves across the apron, allowing flexibility in connecting to various aircraft types. Nose-loader jet bridges connect directly to the front of the aircraft, offering efficient boarding and deboarding. Meanwhile, dual jet bridges provide two access points, enhancing passenger flow and reducing congestion during peak times. Each type is designed to optimize operational efficiency and passenger experience in busy airport environments.</p></p>
