<p><p>The flexible cloth-based sensor market is primarily categorized into pressure sensors and tension sensors. Pressure sensors detect changes in force applied to a surface, making them suitable for applications like wearables and health monitoring. Tension sensors, on the other hand, measure the stretch or pull on materials, useful for robotics and structural health monitoring. Both types leverage flexible fabrics for comfort and adaptability, allowing for seamless integration into everyday clothing and accessories while enhancing user experience and functionality.</p></p>
