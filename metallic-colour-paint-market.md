<p><strong>The Metallic Colour Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic colour paint market is divided into two main types: solvent-based and water-based paints. Solvent-based metallic paints offer excellent durability and weather resistance, making them ideal for outdoor applications. They provide a rich finish but emit higher levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, water-based metallic paints are more environmentally friendly, with lower VOC emissions, and are easier to clean. However, they may offer reduced durability compared to solvent-based options, making them more suitable for indoor use.</p></p>
