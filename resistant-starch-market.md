<p><strong>The Resistant Starch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RS1</li><li>RS2</li><li>RS3</li><li>RS4</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resistant starch market is categorized into four types: RS1, RS2, RS3, and RS4. </p><p>RS1 is found in whole grains and seeds, providing a physical barrier to digestion. RS2 comes from raw potatoes and green bananas, featuring tightly packed granules. RS3, also known as retrograded starch, forms when cooked starchy foods are cooled. Finally, RS4 is chemically modified starch, often used in food processing for specific functional benefits. Each type offers unique health benefits and applications in food industries.</p></p>
