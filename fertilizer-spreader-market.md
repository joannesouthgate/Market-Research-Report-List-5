<p><p>The fertilizer spreader market primarily consists of three types: broadcast spreaders, drop spreaders, and other specialized spreaders. Broadcast spreaders evenly distribute fertilizer over a wide area by flinging granules outward, making them ideal for large lawns and agricultural fields. Drop spreaders, in contrast, release fertilizer directly beneath the hopper, allowing for precise application in targeted areas, which is beneficial for maintaining garden beds and borders. The "other" category includes variations like liquid spreaders and specialty devices tailored for specific crops or applications.</p></p>
