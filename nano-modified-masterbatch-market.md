<p><p>The Nano Modified Masterbatch market includes two primary types based on carrier materials: PP (Polypropylene) Carrier and PE (Polyethylene) Carrier. PP Carrier masterbatch is used for applications requiring high thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it suitable for packaging and automotive parts. In contrast, PE Carrier masterbatch is favored for its flexibility and ease of processing, commonly found in film production and extrusion applications. Both types enhance the material properties of plastics by incorporating nanoscale additives.</p></p>
