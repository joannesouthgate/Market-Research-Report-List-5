<p><p>Boxes in the corrugated market are categorized based on the number of liner and medium layers. Single corrugated boxes consist of one layer of medium sandwiched between two liners, offering basic protection. Double corrugated boxes feature two layers of medium for enhanced strength and durability, suitable for heavier loads. Triple corrugated boxes, with three layers of medium, provide superior sturdiness, ideal for shipping fragile or high-value items. Each type serves distinct packaging needs, balancing cost and protection levels.</p></p>
