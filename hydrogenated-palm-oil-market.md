<p><p>The hydrogenated palm oil market is categorized into two main types: fully hydrogenated palm oil and partially hydrogenated palm oil. Fully hydrogenated palm oil undergoes a complete hydrogenation process, resulting in a stable fat with a longer shelf life, commonly used in baking and confectionery products. In contrast, partially hydrogenated palm oil retains some unsaturated fats, providing unique textures in food but also containing trans fats, which have raised health concerns. Both types serve different food industry applications.</p></p>
