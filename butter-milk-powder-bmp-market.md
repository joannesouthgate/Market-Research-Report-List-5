<p><p>Buttermilk powder (BMP) is a versatile dairy ingredient derived from whey or buttermilk. It is categorized into three main types: Skim Buttermilk Powder, Low-Fat Buttermilk Powder, and Full Cream Buttermilk Powder. Skim Buttermilk Powder contains minimal fat, making it ideal for low-calorie applications. Low-Fat Buttermilk Powder offers a moderate fat content, balancing flavor and health. Full Cream Buttermilk Powder retains the highest fat content, providing rich flavor and creaminess, suitable for culinary and baking uses in various food products.</p></p>
