<p><strong>The Deaerator Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tray-type Deaerator</li><li>Spray-type Deaerator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deaerator systems market is primarily categorized into tray-type and spray-type deaerators. Tray-type deaerators utilize a series of trays to enhance the contact surface between steam and water, promoting efficient removal of dissolved gases. In contrast, spray-type deaerators disperse water into fine droplets, which are then exposed to steam, facilitating rapid gas removal. Both types are vital in industrial applications, particularly in power generation and heating processes, to prevent corrosion and improve system efficiency.</p></p>
