<p><p>The cooling baths market consists of various types used for temperature regulation in laboratory and industrial applications. Ice baths utilize ice and water for moderate cooling. Ice/NaCl baths achieve lower temperatures due to the endothermic reaction between ice and salt. Acetonitrile/dry ice combinations enable extremely low temperatures, suitable for sensitive reactions. Liquid nitrogen/ethanol baths provide rapid and ultra-low cooling, essential for cryogenic applications. Each type serves specific needs based on the required temperature range and application.</p></p>
