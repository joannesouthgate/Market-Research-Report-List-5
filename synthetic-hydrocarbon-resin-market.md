<p><p>The synthetic hydrocarbon resin market is categorized into various types, primarily C5, C9, and other resin types. C5 resins are derived from the polymerization of cyclopentene or piperylene, offering good thermal stability and adhesion properties, making them suitable for adhesives and coatings. C9 resins, originating from mixed aromatic hydrocarbons, are known for their high clarity and excellent chemical resistance, often used in paints and printing inks. Other resins include specialized or modified variants catering to niche applications across industries such as automotive and construction.</p></p>
