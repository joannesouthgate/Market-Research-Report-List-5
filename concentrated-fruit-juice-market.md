<p><strong>The Concentrated Fruit Juice Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbonate Stable</li><li>Clarified</li><li>Alcohol Stable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concentrated fruit juice market comprises three primary types: carbonate stable, clarified, and alcohol stable. Carbonate stable juices maintain carbonation without losing flavor or quality, ideal for soft drinks. Clarified juices undergo a filtration process to remove pulp, resulting in a clear product with a smooth texture, often used in beverages for a refined taste. Alcohol stable juices are specially formulated to withstand fermentation processes, making them suitable for use in alcoholic beverages without compromising flavor integrity.</p></p>
