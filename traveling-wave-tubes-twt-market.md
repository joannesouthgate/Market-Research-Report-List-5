<p><p>Traveling Wave Tubes (TWT) are microwave amplifiers that amplify radio frequency signals using an electron beam and a slow-wave structure. The two primary market types are Helix TWTs, which utilize a helical structure to manage the electron beam while achieving high gain and efficiency, making them suitable for communication satellites and radar systems. Coupled Cavity TWTs consist of multiple cavities that enhance bandwidth and output power, ideal for high-frequency applications and broadcasting. Both play crucial roles in telecommunication and defense sectors.</p></p>
