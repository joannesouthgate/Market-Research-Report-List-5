<p><strong>The Electrosurgical Cautery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Devices</li><li>Bipolar Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrosurgical cautery devices are essential in surgeries for cutting and coagulating tissue. Monopolar devices utilize a single electrode and require grounding pads to complete the circuit, offering versatility for various procedures. In contrast, bipolar devices have two electrodes in close proximity, delivering energy directly to the tissue, which minimizes collateral damage and is ideal for delicate procedures. The market for these devices is driven by advancements in surgical techniques, increasing demand for minimally invasive surgeries, and rising healthcare expenditures.</p></p>
