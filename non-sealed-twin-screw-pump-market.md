<p><strong>The Non-sealed Twin-Screw Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Volute Twin Screw Pump</li><li>Double Volutes Twin Screw Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-sealed twin-screw pump market consists of two primary types: single volute twin-screw pumps and double volute twin-screw pumps. Single volute pumps feature a single flow passage, providing a simple design suitable for low-viscosity fluids with moderate pressure requirements. In contrast, double volute pumps have two flow passages, distributing hydraulic forces more evenly, which minimizes vibration and enhances performance with high-viscosity fluids or higher pressure applications. Both types are essential in various industries for efficient fluid transfer.</p></p>
