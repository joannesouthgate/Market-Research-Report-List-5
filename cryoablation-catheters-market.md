<p><strong>The Cryoablation Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Multielectrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryoablation catheter market features two primary types: single point and multielectrode catheters. Single point cryoablation catheters deliver targeted cold energy to a specific tissue area, ideal for precise ablation in smaller lesions. In contrast, multielectrode catheters utilize multiple points of contact to deliver simultaneous cold energy across a broader tissue surface. This approach facilitates the treatment of larger or more complex lesions, enhancing procedural efficiency and improving outcomes in cardiac and tumor applications.</p></p>
