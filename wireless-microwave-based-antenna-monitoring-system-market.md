<p><p>The wireless microwave-based antenna monitoring system market is categorized into two main types: analog and digital microwave wireless monitoring systems. </p><p>Analog microwave systems transmit continuous signals, providing real-time monitoring of antenna performance without the need for complex processing. In contrast, digital microwave systems convert signals into discrete data packets for enhanced accuracy, storage, and analysis. Both types offer distinct advantages in reliability and performance, catering to diverse applications in telecommunications, broadcasting, and satellite communications, ensuring robust antenna functionality and operational efficiency.</p></p>
