<p><strong>The Hydrogenated Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C5</li><li>C9</li><li>DCPD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogenated resin market is categorized into three main types: C5, C9, and DCPD. C5 hydrogenated resins are derived from pentane-based feedstocks, offering good compatibility with various polymers, primarily used in adhesives and coatings. C9 resins, produced from aromatic hydrocarbons, provide enhanced hardness and adhesion, making them ideal for industrial applications. DCPD (Dicyclopentadiene) resins are known for their high thermal stability and chemical resistance, suitable for automotive and electrical applications. Each type serves unique functions based on their chemical properties and applications.</p></p>
