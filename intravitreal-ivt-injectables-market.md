<p><p>The intravitreal injectables market consists of various treatment types, primarily focusing on conditions affecting the eye. Anti-VEGF agents target abnormal blood vessel growth in diseases like age-related macular degeneration. Corticosteroids reduce inflammation and manage conditions such as diabetic macular edema. Antibiotics treat infections within the eye, while antivirals target viral infections like herpetic eye disease. Antifungals address fungal infections affecting ocular health. Each category plays a critical role in managing ocular diseases, enhancing patient outcomes and preserving vision.</p></p>
