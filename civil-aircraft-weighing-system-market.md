<p><p>The Civil Aircraft Weighing System market is primarily segmented into platform and floor-standing types. Platform systems are designed for weighing aircraft on a flat surface, allowing for accurate load measurement and stability during the process. These systems are often integrated into hangars or maintenance facilities. In contrast, floor-standing systems are self-contained units that can accommodate various aircraft sizes, offering portability and ease of use. Both types ensure compliance with safety regulations and enhance operational efficiency in aircraft maintenance.</p></p>
