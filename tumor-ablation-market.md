<p><p>The tumor ablation market includes various technologies for localized tumor destruction. Radiofrequency (RF) ablation uses heat generated by radio waves to kill cancer cells. Microwave ablation employs microwave energy to create high temperatures for tumor destruction. Cryoablation involves freezing targeted tissue to induce cell death. Other technologies may include laser ablation and high-intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU). Each method offers distinct advantages for treating different tumor types and locations, with varying procedures, efficacy, and potential side effects.</p></p>
