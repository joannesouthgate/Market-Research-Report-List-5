<p><p>The laser displacement sensors market includes two main types: digital laser sensors and CMOS laser sensors. Digital laser sensors provide high precision and reliability for measuring distance or displacement using a digital output, making them ideal for automated applications. Conversely, CMOS laser sensors utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, offering compact designs with lower power consumption, suitable for integration in consumer electronics and various industrial applications. Both types enhance measurement accuracy and efficiency across diverse industries.</p></p>
