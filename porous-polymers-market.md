<p><ul><li>Crystalline State</li><li>Amorphous State</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Porous Polymers Market is categorized based on the polymer's structural state: Crystalline and Amorphous. Crystalline porous polymers have a highly ordered molecular structure, offering precise pore sizes and enhanced stability, making them ideal for applications requiring selectivity, such as gas separation and catalysis. Amorphous porous polymers lack long-range molecular order, providing flexibility and broader pore size distributions, suitable for applications like adsorption and filtration. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, balancing structural precision and functional versatility.</p></p>
