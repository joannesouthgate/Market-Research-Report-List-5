<p><p>The oats ingredient market can be categorized into three types: Croats, Regular/Flakes, and Powder/Flour. Croats are coarsely ground oats, often used in bakery products for texture. Regular oats, also known as rolled flakes, are steamed and flattened, making them suitable for quick cooking and commonly used in breakfast cereals. Powder or flour oats are finely ground, ideal for baking and as thickening agents in various recipes. Each type serves distinct culinary uses, appealing to a broad range of consumer preferences and dietary needs.</p></p>
