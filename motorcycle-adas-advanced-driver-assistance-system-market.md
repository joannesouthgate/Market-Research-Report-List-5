<p><p>Motorcycle Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) enhance rider safety and control through various technologies. The Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lock during braking, ensuring stability. Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) adjusts speed to maintain safe distances from other vehicles. Gear Shift Assist facilitates smooth gear transitions without the clutch. Tire-Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) alerts riders to changes in tire pressure, preventing blowouts. Traction Control System (TCS) minimizes wheel spin during acceleration, improving stability and grip on slippery surfaces.</p></p>
