<p><p>The automotive in-vehicle air purifier market includes three main types: air purifiers, air ionizers, and hybrid systems. Air purifiers typically use HEPA filters to capture particulate matter and pollutants, enhancing air quality. Air ionizers work by charging particles in the air, causing them to settle out of circulation and reducing airborne contaminants. Hybrid systems combine both technologies to maximize air purification efficiency, offering comprehensive solutions for removing odors, allergens, and harmful substances within vehicle interiors.</p></p>
