<p><p>The subwoofers market is primarily divided into two types: powered subwoofers and passive subwoofers. Powered subwoofers come with a built-in amplifier, making them easier to set up and ideal for home theaters or music systems. They offer enhanced bass performance and require just a power source and audio input. On the other hand, passive subwoofers do not have an integrated amplifier, requiring an external amplifier for operation. They are often favored by enthusiasts seeking customization and higher sound quality.</p></p>
