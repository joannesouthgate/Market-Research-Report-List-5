<p><strong>The Nuclear Imaging Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positron Emission Tomography</li><li>Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography</li><li>Planar Scintigraphy Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear imaging systems market comprises three key types: Positron Emission Tomography (PET), Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT), and Planar Scintigraphy Systems. PET utilizes radioactive tracers to visualize metabolic processes, making it valuable for cancer and brain disorders. SPECT employs gamma rays to produce detailed 3D images, aiding in cardiac and neurological assessments. Planar Scintigraphy offers 2D images, primarily used for evaluating organ function and detecting abnormalities. Collectively, these technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning in healthcare.</p></p>
