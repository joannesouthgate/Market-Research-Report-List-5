<p><ul><li>Self-Supporting (Free-Standing) Tower</li><li>Guyed Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind energy equipment market features two prominent tower types: self-supporting (free-standing) towers and guyed towers. Self-supporting towers are robust structures that require no external support, making them ideal for locations with limited space or harsh conditions. In contrast, guyed towers utilize guy wires for stabilization, allowing for taller constructions at a lower cost. Both types are essential in harnessing wind energy efficiently, with choices dependent on factors like wind conditions, site accessibility, and budget considerations.</p></p>
