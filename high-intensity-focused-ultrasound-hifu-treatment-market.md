<p><p>The High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) treatment market comprises two main types: ultrasound-guided and MR-guided systems. Ultrasound-guided HIFU utilizes real-time ultrasound imaging to target tissues accurately, making it effective for various applications like ablations. In contrast, MR-guided HIFU combines magnetic resonance imaging with focused ultrasound to enhance precision, allowing for detailed visualization of anatomy and monitoring treatment effects. Both modalities are increasingly popular for non-invasive treatments in areas such as oncology and cosmetic procedures.</p></p>
