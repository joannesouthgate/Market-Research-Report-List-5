<p><p>The Aluminum Alloy Automotive Wheels market includes **Forging Aluminum Alloy Wheels** and **Casting Aluminum Alloy Wheels**. Forged wheels are made by compressing aluminum under high pressure, resulting in superior strength, lighter weight, and enhanced performance, making them ideal for high-performance and luxury vehicles. Cast wheels are produced by pouring molten aluminum into molds, offering cost-effectiveness, design flexibility, and suitability for mass-market vehicles. While forged wheels excel in durability and performance, cast wheels are more affordable and widely used in standard automotive applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
