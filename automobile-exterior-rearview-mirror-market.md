<p><p>The automobile exterior rearview mirror market is primarily segmented into two types: plane mirrors and convex mirrors. Plane mirrors provide a true-to-life reflection, offering a clear view of objects directly behind the vehicle, making them suitable for precise maneuvers. In contrast, convex mirrors have a curved surface that expands the field of view, allowing drivers to see a wider area, including blind spots. Both types serve essential safety functions, enhancing visibility and awareness while driving.</p></p>
