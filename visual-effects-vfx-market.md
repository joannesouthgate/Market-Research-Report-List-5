<p><p>The Visual Effects (VFX) market encompasses various segments, including Simulation FX, Animation, Modelling, Matte Painting, and Compositing. Simulation FX involves creating realistic physical phenomena like fire, water, and explosions. Animation focuses on bringing characters and objects to life through movement. Modelling entails crafting 3D representations of objects and characters. Matte Painting creates detailed backgrounds, merging painted imagery with live action. Compositing combines visual elements from different sources into a single cohesive image, enhancing the overall visual storytelling.</p></p>
