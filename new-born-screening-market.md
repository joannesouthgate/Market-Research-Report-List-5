<p><p>Newborn screening encompasses various technologies to identify potential health issues early. Tandem mass spectrometry analyzes metabolites to detect metabolic disorders. Pulse oximetry screening measures oxygen levels in the blood to identify congenital heart defects. DNA-based assays utilize genetic testing to pinpoint inherited conditions. Hearing screening technology assesses auditory function to detect congenital hearing loss. Other methods may include behavioral assessments and biochemical tests. Together, these approaches ensure timely interventions, improving newborn health outcomes and reducing long-term complications.</p></p>
