<p><strong>The Veterinary Thermography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wavelength Thermography Cameras</li><li>Mid-wavelength Thermography Cameras</li><li>Long-wavelength Thermography Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Veterinary thermography encompasses three main camera types used to assess animal health through thermal imaging. Short-wavelength thermography cameras detect heat in the visible to near-infrared spectrum, useful for surface temperature analysis. Mid-wavelength cameras operate within the thermal infrared range, effective for monitoring physiological changes. Long-wavelength thermography cameras focus on the far-infrared spectrum, ideal for deeper tissue examination. Each type offers unique advantages for identifying inflammation, injuries, or other health issues in animals, enhancing veterinary diagnostics and treatment planning.</p></p>
