<p><p>The COP wafer market can be categorized into two main types: Low COP wafers and COP-free wafers. Low COP wafers are characterized by a lower coefficient of thermal expansion, which enhances their stability in high-temperature applications, making them suitable for advanced packaging technologies. On the other hand, COP-free wafers are designed to eliminate the use of cyclic olefin polymers, offering alternative materials with distinct properties that may cater to specific manufacturing needs and environmental considerations. Both types cater to diverse industry requirements.</p></p>
