<p><p>Embedded vision cameras are crucial in industrial automation, robotics, and other applications. The market is primarily segmented into area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture an entire image in a single exposure, making them ideal for applications requiring high-resolution images of stationary scenes. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, which is advantageous for inspecting moving products or continuous processes, offering high-speed data acquisition in production lines.</p></p>
