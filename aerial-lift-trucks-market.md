<p><p>The aerial lift truck market comprises three main types: scissor lifts, boom lifts, and telehandlers. Scissor lifts provide vertical elevation with a compact design, ideal for indoor use and tight spaces. Boom lifts extend both vertically and horizontally, offering flexibility for accessing hard-to-reach areas, making them suitable for construction and maintenance tasks. Telehandlers combine the features of a forklift and aerial lift, providing versatility for lifting and transporting materials at various heights, commonly used in agriculture and construction settings.</p></p>
