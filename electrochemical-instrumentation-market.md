<p><p>The electrochemical instrumentation market encompasses various types of devices used for analyzing and measuring electrochemical parameters. Potentiostats and galvanostats control and measure voltage/current in electrochemical cells. Ion chromatographs separate ions for precise concentration analysis. Conductivity and salinity meters assess solution conductivity and salt levels, respectively. Titrators determine the concentration of substances through chemical reaction. pH meters measure acidity or alkalinity in solutions. Other instruments may include sensors and analyzers for specialized applications in research and industry.</p></p>
