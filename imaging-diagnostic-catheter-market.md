<p><strong>The Imaging Diagnostic Catheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Angiography Catheters</li><li>Ultrasound Catheters</li><li>OCT Catheters</li><li>Electrophysiology (EP) Catheters</li><li>Other Imaging Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The imaging diagnostic catheter market encompasses various types designed for specific medical imaging applications. Angiography catheters facilitate visualization of blood vessels during angiograms. Ultrasound catheters utilize sound waves for real-time imaging of internal structures. OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) catheters provide high-resolution imaging, particularly in ophthalmology and cardiovascular settings. Electrophysiology (EP) catheters are used to map and treat electrical activity in the heart. Other imaging catheters may include specialized devices for unique diagnostic purposes, expanding the capabilities in clinical settings.</p></p>
