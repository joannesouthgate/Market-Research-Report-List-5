<p><p>Vacuum Insulation Board (VIB) is an advanced thermal insulation material composed of a core material, typically fumed silica or fiberglass, enclosed in a gas-tight envelope from which air is evacuated to create a vacuum. This structure minimizes heat transfer, making VIB highly effective for applications requiring superior thermal performance, such as in construction, refrigeration, and transportation. Its high insulation efficiency, lightweight nature, and slim profile make it ideal for space-constrained applications.</p><p>The Vacuum Insulation Board Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient solutions and stringent building regulations aimed at reducing carbon emissions.
