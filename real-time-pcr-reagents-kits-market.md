<p><p>The Real-Time PCR reagents and kits market is segmented into two main types: One-step Real-Time PCR and Two-step Real-Time PCR. One-step Real-Time PCR involves a single reaction where reverse transcription and amplification occur simultaneously, allowing for quick and efficient analysis. In contrast, Two-step Real-Time PCR separates these processes into two distinct steps, offering greater flexibility and specificity in quantifying RNA. Each type caters to different research and diagnostic needs, contributing to the market's diverse applications.</p></p>
