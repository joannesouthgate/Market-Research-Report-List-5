<p><p>The miniature power supply market consists primarily of AC-DC and DC-DC supply types. AC-DC power supplies convert alternating current from mains sources into direct current, suitable for powering various electronic devices and applications. They are commonly used in consumer electronics and industrial equipment. On the other hand, DC-DC power supplies convert one level of direct current to another, allowing for voltage regulation and optimization in battery-operated devices and embedded systems. Both types are essential for efficient energy management in modern electronics.</p></p>
