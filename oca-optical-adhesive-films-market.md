<p><p>The OCA (Optically Clear Adhesive) market is segmented into substrate-free and substrate optical adhesives. Substrate-free optical adhesives are utilized to bond components without a backing material, favoring applications requiring high clarity and flexibility, such as touchscreens and displays. In contrast, substrate optical adhesives include a backing layer, offering enhanced stability and ease of handling in assembly processes. Both types are crucial for industries like consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices, ensuring optimal optical performance and mechanical reliability.</p></p>
