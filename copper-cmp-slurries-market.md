<p><strong>The Copper CMP Slurries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prestonian Type</li><li>Non-Prestonian Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) slurries are essential in semiconductor manufacturing for achieving smooth surfaces on copper interconnects. The market can be segmented into Prestonian and Non-Prestonian types. Prestonian slurries exhibit a direct relationship between removal rate and applied pressure, making them effective for controlled polishing. Non-Prestonian slurries, on the other hand, have a removal rate that is less dependent on pressure, often offering greater versatility and lower defect rates. Both types cater to specific manufacturing needs and process requirements.</p></p>
