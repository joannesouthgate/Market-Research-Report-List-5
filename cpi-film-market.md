<p><p>CPI film market is categorized into two main types: double-layer and monolayer films. Double-layer films consist of two distinct polymer layers, offering enhanced properties such as improved barrier performance and durability, making them ideal for various protective applications. In contrast, monolayer films are composed of a single polymer layer, providing basic functionality and cost-effectiveness, typically used in simpler packaging solutions. Both types serve specific needs in packaging, branded goods, and industrial applications, reflecting diverse market demands.</p></p>
