<p><strong>The Enzyme Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidoreductases</li><li>Transferases</li><li>Hydrolases</li><li>Isomerases</li><li>Lyases</li><li>Ligases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The enzyme market is categorized into six main types: Oxidoreductases, which facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions; Transferases, responsible for transferring functional groups; Hydrolases, which catalyze the breakdown of compounds through hydrolysis; Isomerases, that rearrange molecular structures; Lyases, which remove or add groups without hydrolysis; and Ligases, which join molecules by forming bonds, often with the help of ATP. These enzyme types play critical roles across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and food processing, driving innovation and growth.</p></p>
