<p><p>The quantitative immunoassay market comprises various types, prominently featuring electrochemiluminescence immunoassays (ECLIA) and bead-based immunoassays. ECLIA utilizes electrochemiluminescent labels for high sensitivity and specificity, making it ideal for detecting low concentration biomarkers. Bead-based immunoassays leverage the use of microbeads to capture and detect analytes, allowing multiplexing and simultaneous analysis of multiple targets. Both methods enhance diagnostic accuracy and efficiency, significantly contributing to advancements in clinical laboratories and research settings within the healthcare sector.</p></p>
