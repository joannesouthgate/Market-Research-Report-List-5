<p><p>The colostomy bags market is primarily divided into two types: the one-piece system and the two-piece system. The one-piece system integrates the adhesive skin barrier and the pouch into a single unit, offering ease of use and a discreet appearance. Conversely, the two-piece system features a separate adhesive barrier and pouch, allowing for more flexibility in changing the pouch while keeping the barrier in place. Both systems cater to different user preferences and needs regarding comfort, convenience, and lifestyle.</p></p>
