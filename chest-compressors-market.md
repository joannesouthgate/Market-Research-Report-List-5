<p><strong>The Chest Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chest compressors market encompasses mechanical and electric types, each designed to aid in cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) for patients in cardiac arrest. Mechanical chest compressors utilize a system of levers and springs to deliver consistent compressions, reducing the physical strain on rescuers. In contrast, electric chest compressors use motors and electronic controls to provide adjustable compression depth and rate, enhancing effectiveness during emergencies. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes by ensuring high-quality, uninterrupted CPR delivery.</p></p>
