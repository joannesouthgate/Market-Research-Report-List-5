<p><strong>The Lithium Metal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Salt Lake Brine</li><li>Lithium Ore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium metal market is primarily divided into two types: salt lake brine and lithium ore. Salt lake brine involves extracting lithium from saline water found in salt flats, utilizing evaporation processes. This method is often more cost-effective and sustainable. Conversely, lithium ore extraction focuses on mining hard rock deposits, primarily spodumene, which is more resource-intensive but can yield higher purity lithium. Both methods serve critical roles in supplying lithium for batteries and other applications in the growing energy sector.</p></p>
