<p><p>The Radiation Curing Adhesive market is primarily categorized into two types: Ultraviolet (UV) curing and Electron Beam (EB) curing. UV curing involves exposing adhesives to ultraviolet light, which initiates a rapid curing process, providing benefits like quick drying and strong bond formation. In contrast, EB curing utilizes high-energy electron beams to achieve polymerization, offering advantages such as deep penetration and enhanced performance in challenging environments. Both methods are widely used in industries like automotive, electronics, and packaging for efficient and durable bonding solutions.</p></p>
