<p><p>The market for LED driving power supplies is primarily categorized into two types: external power supplies and built-in power supplies. External power supplies are standalone units designed to deliver power to LEDs from a separate source, offering flexibility in design and easy replacement. In contrast, built-in power supplies are integrated within the LED fixture, providing a compact solution that simplifies installation and reduces external wiring. Each type caters to different applications and preferences in the lighting industry.</p></p>
