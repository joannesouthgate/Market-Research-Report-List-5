<p><ul><li>"Capital Lease"</li><li>"Operating Lease"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rental construction equipment market primarily comprises two lease types: capital leases and operating leases. A capital lease allows the lessee to acquire equipment with the intent of ownership, often leading to lower long-term costs, as payments contribute to asset ownership. In contrast, an operating lease offers a shorter-term rental solution without ownership transfer, providing flexibility and lower initial costs, making it ideal for projects with temporary equipment needs. Both lease types cater to different financial strategies in construction projects.</p></p>
