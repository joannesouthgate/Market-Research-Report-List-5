<p><strong>The Nuclear Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>No-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear battery market is divided into two main types: thermal conversion and non-thermal conversion. </p><p>Thermal conversion types generate energy by utilizing the heat produced from radioactive decay, converting it into electricity through thermoelectric materials. </p><p>In contrast, non-thermal conversion types harness direct energy from radiation, typically utilizing radioactive isotopes to create electrical energy without the need for heat conversion. Both types serve various applications, including space exploration, remote sensing, and medical devices, highlighting their unique advantages and use cases.</p></p>
