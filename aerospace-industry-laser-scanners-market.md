<p><p>The aerospace industry utilizes laser scanners to enhance measurement precision and quality control. Non-contact type laser scanners use light beams to capture detailed surface data without physical contact, making them ideal for delicate components and minimizing damage risk. In contrast, contact type laser scanners involve direct contact with the object being scanned, providing highly accurate measurements but potentially affecting sensitive surfaces. Both types play crucial roles in manufacturing, inspection, and maintenance processes, contributing to improved efficiency and reliability in aerospace applications.</p></p>
