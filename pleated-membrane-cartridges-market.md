<p><p>The pleated membrane cartridges market consists of various types designed for different filtration needs. PP filters, made from polypropylene, are known for their chemical resistance and efficiency in removing particulates. PES filters, made from polyethersulfone, offer high flow rates and thermal stability, suitable for biological applications. PTFE filters are hydrophobic, ideal for gas filtration and aggressive chemicals. Nylon filters provide robust performance, particularly in aqueous solutions. Other filters encompass specialty membranes catering to niche applications, enhancing versatility in the market.</p></p>
