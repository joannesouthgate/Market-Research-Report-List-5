<p><p>The Laser Direct Structuring (LDS) resin market comprises various materials, each suited for specific applications. PA (polyamide) offers durability and flexibility, while PC (polycarbonate) provides high impact resistance. ABS (acrylonitrile butadiene styrene) is known for its toughness and ease of processing. PC/ABS combines the strengths of both polycarbonate and ABS. PPA (polyphthalamide) offers chemical resistance, and LCP (liquid crystal polymer) features excellent thermal stability. Other materials like PBT (polybutylene terephthalate) and COP (cyclo-olefin polymer) are also used, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
