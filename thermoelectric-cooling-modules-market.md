<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multi Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoelectric cooling modules are categorized into single-stage and multi-stage types based on their cooling capacity and application. Single-stage modules utilize a single thermoelectric element to achieve moderate temperature reductions, making them suitable for compact applications like portable coolers or electronic device cooling. In contrast, multi-stage modules consist of stacked thermoelectric elements, allowing for greater temperature differentials and enhanced cooling performance, ideal for industrial processes or precise temperature control in medical and scientific equipment.</p></p>
