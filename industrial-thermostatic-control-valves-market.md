<p><ul><li>3-way Thermostatic Control Valves</li><li>2-way Thermostatic Control Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial thermostatic control valves market comprises mainly two types: 3-way and 2-way thermostatic control valves. 3-way thermostatic control valves are designed to regulate the flow of fluids by allowing mixing or diverting, making them suitable for applications requiring precise temperature control. In contrast, 2-way thermostatic control valves manage flow by opening and closing, providing straightforward temperature regulation in heating and cooling systems. Both types are essential for enhancing energy efficiency and ensuring optimal performance in industrial processes.</p></p>
