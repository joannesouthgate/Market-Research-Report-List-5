<p><p>The in-line valves market comprises three primary types: manual valves, pneumatic valves, and electromagnetic valves. Manual valves require physical operation, allowing users to control flow directly, often used in simple applications. Pneumatic valves utilize compressed air for automation, providing quick actuation for high-speed applications and are widely used in industrial settings. Electromagnetic valves operate using an electric current to open or close, enabling precise control in systems like irrigation or HVAC. Each type serves distinct operational needs and applications.</p></p>
