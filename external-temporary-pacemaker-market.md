<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The External Temporary Pacemaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Chamber External Temporary Pacemaker</li><li>Single Chamber External Temporary Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The external temporary pacemaker market comprises two main types: dual chamber and single chamber external temporary pacemakers. A single chamber external temporary pacemaker stimulates either the right atrium or right ventricle, providing basic pacing support, commonly used for acute arrhythmias. In contrast, a dual chamber external temporary pacemaker stimulates both the atrium and ventricle, ensuring coordinated heartbeats and improving hemodynamic stability. These devices are typically used in critical care settings for short-term pacing needs.</p></p>
