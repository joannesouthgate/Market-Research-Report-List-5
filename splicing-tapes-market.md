Repulpable splicing tapes are designed for use in paper and other flexible substrates, allowing them to be easily integrated into the recycling process, enhancing sustainability. In contrast, non-repulpable splicing tapes are typically used in applications where immediate strength and adhesion are crucial, providing robust performance without the need for recyclability.
