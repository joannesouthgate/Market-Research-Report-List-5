<p><p>Mass flow controllers (MFCs) for gases are essential tools that regulate the flow rate of gases in various applications. The market can be categorized into direct and indirect types. Direct MFCs measure and control the flow rate directly using a sensor, providing high accuracy and response times. In contrast, indirect MFCs rely on estimated flow calculations based on pressure and temperature measurements, making them less precise but often more cost-effective. Each type serves distinct needs in industrial processes and research.</p></p>
