<p><strong>The Reducing Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weld-Neck Reducing Flanges</li><li>Slip-On Reducing Flanges</li><li>Threaded Reducing Flanges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reducing flanges market comprises various types designed for different applications. Weld-neck reducing flanges feature a long taper for strong welding, suitable for high-pressure systems. Slip-on reducing flanges allow easy alignment and installation, making them ideal for low-pressure scenarios. Threaded reducing flanges are designed with internal threads for easy connection to pipes without welding, suited for situations where disassembly is frequent. Each type caters to specific requirements in industrial piping systems, enhancing efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
