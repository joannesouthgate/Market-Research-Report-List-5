<p><strong>The Perfusion Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Computed Tomography (CT Scan)</li><li>Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)</li><li>Nuclear Medicine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Perfusion imaging assesses blood flow in tissues, crucial for diagnosing various medical conditions. The market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Computed Tomography (CT) Scan**: Utilizes X-rays to create detailed cross-sectional images, highlighting blood flow dynamics and abnormalities.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)**: Employs magnetic fields and radio waves to produce high-resolution images, advantageous for soft tissue visualization and functional assessment.</p><p>  </p><p>3. **Nuclear Medicine**: Involves radioactive tracers to evaluate blood perfusion, providing insights into metabolic activity and organ function. Each modality enhances diagnostic capabilities and aids treatment planning.</p></p>
