<p><p>Floating Roof Aboveground Storage Tanks (ASTs) are specialized tanks designed for the storage of liquids, primarily fuels, while minimizing evaporation losses and emissions. These tanks feature a roof that floats on the surface of the stored liquid, adjusting its height to maintain close proximity as the liquid levels change. This design prevents the accumulation of vapor space above the liquid, significantly reducing volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and enhancing safety.</p><p>The Floating Roof AST Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by escalating demand for efficient storage solutions in the oil and gas sector, increased environmental regulations emphasizing emission reductions, and the rising need for energy security.
