<p><ul><li>Surface Mount Technology</li><li>Through Hole Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Reliability (HiRel) semiconductors market consists of two primary types: Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Through Hole Technology (THT). SMT involves mounting components directly onto the surface of PCBs, offering high density and efficiency, making it ideal for compact and advanced applications. In contrast, THT involves inserting leads through drilled holes in the PCB, providing robust mechanical connections, which are essential for demanding environments. Both technologies cater to sectors requiring high reliability, such as aerospace, defense, and medical devices.</p></p>
