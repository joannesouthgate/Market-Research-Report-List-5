<p><p>The IoT Wireless Sensor Market comprises various technologies, including Traditional Wireless Technologies and LPWANs (Low Power Wide Area Networks). Traditional Wireless Technologies, such as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, offer higher data rates and short-range communication, suitable for applications requiring immediate data exchange. In contrast, LPWANs are designed for long-range connectivity with low power consumption, ideal for devices that transmit small amounts of data over extended periods, making them perfect for remote monitoring and smart city applications. Both technologies cater to distinct IoT needs.</p></p>
