<p><p>Biologic products in the nerve repair market include nerve conduits, nerve wraps, and nerve grafts. Nerve conduits serve as hollow tubes that guide regenerating nerves, facilitating proper alignment and healing. Nerve wraps are designed to encase and protect damaged nerves, promoting recovery and preventing scarring. Nerve grafts involve transplanting healthy nerve tissue to bridge gaps in injured nerves, enabling functional restoration. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing nerve regeneration and restoring nerve function following injury or trauma.</p></p>
