InSb offers high sensitivity and fast response times, making it suitable for applications like thermal imaging and spectroscopy. PbSe is favored for its spectral range and is commonly used in gas detection and environmental monitoring. PbS is ideal for near-infrared applications, particularly in detecting low levels of infrared radiation, commonly used in industrial and military applications.</p></p>
