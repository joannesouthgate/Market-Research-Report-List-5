<p><p>The Suspension-by-Wire market encompasses advanced technologies like Active Roll Control (ARC) and Adaptive Damping System (ADS). ARC enhances vehicle stability by actively adjusting suspension settings to counteract body roll during cornering, improving handling and passenger comfort. On the other hand, ADS monitors road conditions in real-time, adapting the suspension stiffness for optimal ride quality. Both systems contribute to enhanced vehicle performance, safety, and comfort, representing a shift towards more integrated and electronically controlled suspension solutions in modern automotive design.</p></p>
