<p><p>The automotive rod antenna market includes two primary types: active rod antennas and passive rod antennas. Active rod antennas incorporate electronic components to boost signal reception and transmission, enhancing overall performance, especially in areas with weak signals. In contrast, passive rod antennas lack these electronics, relying solely on their design and placement to capture signals, making them simpler and cost-effective. Both types serve essential roles in vehicle communication systems, contributing to navigation, entertainment, and safety features.</p></p>
