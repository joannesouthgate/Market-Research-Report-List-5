<p><p>The healthcare arrhythmia monitoring devices market comprises several types, each serving distinct needs. Resting ECG devices capture heart rhythms in a stationary setting, while ECG stress test devices assess cardiac responses during physical exertion. Holter monitors provide continuous heart monitoring over 24 to 48 hours, aiding in identifying intermittent issues. Implantable cardiac monitors offer long-term monitoring for arrhythmias, discreetly inserted under the skin. Additional devices include event recorders and mobile cardiac telemetry, enhancing arrhythmia detection and management capabilities.</p></p>
