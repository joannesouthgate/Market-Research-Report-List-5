<p><p>The automatic toilet paper dispenser market can be divided into two main types: contact and non-contact dispensers. Contact toilet paper dispensers require users to physically touch the device to obtain paper, often seen in budget-friendly settings. In contrast, non-contact toilet paper dispensers utilize sensors to dispense paper without physical contact, enhancing hygiene and reducing cross-contamination. These dispensers are increasingly preferred in high-traffic public restrooms and healthcare facilities, reflecting a growing emphasis on health and cleanliness in restroom environments.</p></p>
