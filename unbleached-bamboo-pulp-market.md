<p><strong>The Unbleached Bamboo Pulp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Caustic Soda Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The unbleached bamboo pulp market primarily consists of two production processes: the sulfate process and the caustic soda process. The sulfate process, also known as the kraft process, utilizes sodium sulfate to dissolve lignin, resulting in strong and durable pulp, suitable for various paper products. In contrast, the caustic soda process employs sodium hydroxide to extract cellulose, producing a pulp that is generally softer and has a lighter color. Both processes cater to the growing demand for sustainable and eco-friendly paper products.</p></p>
