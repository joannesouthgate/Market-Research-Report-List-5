<p><p>The Food and Beverage Metal Cans market is primarily divided into two types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are constructed from a circular body, along with two separate ends, typically welded or soldered together. They are often used for products like soup and vegetables. Two-piece cans, on the other hand, are made from a single sheet of metal, formed into a cylindrical shape with a seamless body and a lid, commonly used for beverages and some food items, offering a more efficient and modern design.</p></p>
