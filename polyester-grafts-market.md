<p><p>The polyester grafts market features two primary types: knitted polyester vascular grafts and woven polyester vascular grafts. Knitted polyester grafts are constructed using interlocking threads, providing flexibility and enhanced compliance, making them suitable for various vascular applications. In contrast, woven polyester grafts are made from tightly woven fibers, offering superior strength and durability, ideal for high-pressure vascular areas. Both types are used in surgical procedures to replace or repair damaged arteries, with specific characteristics catering to different patient needs and surgical requirements.</p></p>
