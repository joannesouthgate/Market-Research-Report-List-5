<p><strong>The High Voltage Switchgear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS)</li><li>Air Insulated Switchgear (AIS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high voltage switchgear market is primarily divided into two types: Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS) and Air Insulated Switchgear (AIS). GIS is known for its compact design and ability to function in limited spaces, utilizing insulating gas to enhance performance and reliability, making it suitable for urban environments. Conversely, AIS employs air as the primary insulation medium, typically requiring more space but offering ease of maintenance and lower initial costs, making it ideal for substations and outdoor installations.</p></p>
