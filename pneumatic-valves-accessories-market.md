<p><ul><li>Sliding Shaft</li><li>Rotating Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic valves and accessories are essential components in controlling airflow and pressure in pneumatic systems. The market can be segmented into two main types: sliding shaft and rotating shaft valves. Sliding shaft valves operate by moving a shaft linearly to open or close the airflow path, offering precise control in applications like actuators. Conversely, rotating shaft valves utilize a rotary motion to manage airflow, ideal for applications requiring smooth and continuous flow. Both types enhance system efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
