<p><p>Polycarbonate filter lenses are categorized into three main types based on their light filtration capabilities: weak, neutral, and strong filters. Weak filters reduce light intensity slightly, providing a mild protective effect, ideal for low-light conditions. Neutral filters balance color across the spectrum without altering hues, making them suitable for various environments. Strong filters significantly decrease light transmission, protecting against intense light exposure, commonly used in bright outdoor settings or specific industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs in vision safety and comfort.</p></p>
