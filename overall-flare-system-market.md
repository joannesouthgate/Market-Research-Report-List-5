<p><ul><li>Air Assisted Flares</li><li>Steam Assisted Flares</li><li>Pressure Assisted Flares</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overall flare system market includes three primary types: air-assisted, steam-assisted, and pressure-assisted flares. Air-assisted flares utilize ambient air to enhance combustion, making them effective for lower volumetric flow rates. Steam-assisted flares inject steam to improve combustion efficiency and reduce smoke emissions, suitable for high-capacity operations. Pressure-assisted flares rely on pressurized gas to forcefully mix fuel and air, ensuring complete combustion, which is ideal for situations requiring high reliability and efficiency. Each type caters to specific operational needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
