<p><p>Smart textiles for wearable technology are classified into three market types: passive smart textiles, active smart textiles, and ultra-smart textiles. Passive smart textiles sense environmental conditions but do not respond actively, providing basic data like temperature or moisture. Active smart textiles can react to stimuli, adjusting properties in real time, such as changing color or regulating temperature. Ultra-smart textiles incorporate advanced technologies, enabling them to process information, communicate, and learn from the environment, offering dynamic interaction and adaptability for complex applications.</p></p>
