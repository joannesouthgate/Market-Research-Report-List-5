<p><ul><li>Rigid Nephroscope</li><li>Flexible Nephroscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nephroscope market comprises two main types: rigid nephroscopes and flexible nephroscopes. Rigid nephroscopes are inflexible instruments that provide excellent visual clarity and are primarily used for procedures like stone removal and biopsy. In contrast, flexible nephroscopes can navigate the renal anatomy more easily, allowing for less invasive procedures and improved patient comfort. Both types serve critical roles in urology, enabling precise diagnosis and treatment of kidney-related conditions, thus driving their demand in the medical device market.</p></p>
