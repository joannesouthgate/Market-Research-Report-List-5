<p><p>The general anesthesia drugs market is primarily divided into two categories: intravenous anesthetics and inhalational anesthetics. Intravenous anesthetics are administered through injection, providing rapid sedation and analgesia, making them ideal for short procedures and induction phases. Inhalational anesthetics, delivered via a gas mixture, allow for controlled sedation during longer surgeries and facilitate maintenance of anesthesia. Both categories are essential in surgical settings, enhancing patient comfort and safety while enabling healthcare providers to perform complex medical procedures effectively.</p></p>
