<p><p>The panoramic dental X-ray equipment market is categorized into two main types: digital and analog. Digital panoramic X-rays utilize digital sensors to capture images, offering higher resolution, faster processing, and easier storage and retrieval. In contrast, analog panoramic X-rays use traditional film-based technology, which often requires longer exposure times and physical film handling. Digital systems are increasingly preferred due to their efficiency, lower radiation exposure, and enhanced image quality, driving a trend toward digital solutions in dental practices.</p></p>
