<p><ul><li>Differential scanning calorimeter</li><li>Oxygen bomb calorimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic calorimeter market includes various types, primarily the differential scanning calorimeter (DSC) and the oxygen bomb calorimeter. DSC measures heat flow changes in materials as they undergo temperature variations, providing insights into thermal properties and phase transitions. In contrast, the oxygen bomb calorimeter determines the calorific value of combustible substances by measuring heat release during combustion in a high-pressure environment. Both instruments play crucial roles in research, quality control, and materials testing across industries.</p></p>
