<p><p>Suction diffusers are critical components in HVAC and industrial systems, designed to improve airflow and efficiency. There are two primary market types: integral vane and separate vane diffusers. Integral vane diffusers feature built-in vanes that guide the airflow smoothly into the system, minimizing turbulence and energy loss. In contrast, separate vane diffusers utilize externally mounted vanes, allowing for greater flexibility in design and adjustment. Both types enhance system performance, reduce noise, and contribute to energy savings in air handling applications.</p></p>
