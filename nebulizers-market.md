<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nebulizers</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebulizers</li><li>Mesh Nebulizers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nebulizer market comprises several types, including pneumatic, ultrasonic, mesh, and others. Pneumatic nebulizers, also known as jet nebulizers, use compressed air to convert liquid medication into aerosol droplets. Ultrasonic nebulizers employ high-frequency vibrations to create mist, making them quieter and more portable. Mesh nebulizers utilize a vibrating mesh to produce aerosol, offering efficient drug delivery and reduced medication waste. Other types may include portable nebulizers and breathe-actuated devices, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
