<p><p>Press type welders encompass various techniques for joining metal components through localized heating and pressure. Projection welding uses raised sections of the workpieces, allowing for efficient, multi-point connections, ideal for automating assembly lines. Spot welding, on the other hand, focuses on joining two metal sheets at specific points using electrode pressure and electrical current, making it prevalent in automotive manufacturing. Both methods enhance productivity and precision, driving growth in industries requiring robust metal joining solutions.</p></p>
