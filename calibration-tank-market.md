<p><p>The calibration tank market consists of various types designed for specific applications. Pipe calibration tanks are large vessels used for calibrating flow meters and ensuring accurate measurement in pipeline systems. They accommodate varying pipe sizes and enable controlled flow rates. Squeeze calibration tanks, on the other hand, utilize a squeeze mechanism to provide precise flow rates for testing and calibration purposes. Both types ensure the accurate measurement of liquids, crucial for industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
