<p><ul><li>Colostomy Drainage Bags</li><li>Ileostomy Drainage Bags</li><li>Urostomy Drainage Bags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ostomy devices market encompasses three primary types: colostomy drainage bags, ileostomy drainage bags, and urostomy drainage bags. Colostomy bags are used for patients who have had a segment of the colon removed, collecting waste from the remaining colon. Ileostomy bags serve similar purposes for those with small intestine surgery, collecting digestive waste. Urostomy bags are designed for individuals who have undergone urinary diversion, collecting urine directly from the bladder. Each type addresses specific medical needs for better patient comfort and hygiene.</p></p>
