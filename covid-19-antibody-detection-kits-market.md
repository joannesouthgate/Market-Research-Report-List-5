<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 antibody detection kits market comprises three main types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs), which provide quick results and are user-friendly, making them suitable for point-of-care settings; Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), which offer higher sensitivity and specificity, typically used in laboratory environments for detailed analysis; and Neutralization Assays, which assess the functional ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus, crucial for understanding immunity and vaccine efficacy. Each type serves different diagnostic and research needs in the pandemic response.</p></p>
