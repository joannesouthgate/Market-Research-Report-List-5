<p><p>The genetic disease diagnostic market comprises various segments. Prenatal diagnosis involves testing fetuses for genetic disorders using methods like amniocentesis and chorionic villus sampling. Screening for single-gene genetic diseases focuses on identifying specific inherited conditions, often through targeted genetic tests. Preimplantation genetic testing assesses embryos for genetic defects before implantation during in vitro fertilization. Other diagnostic methods may include carrier screening, newborn screening, and advanced technologies like whole genome sequencing, catering to diverse genetic testing needs and conditions.</p></p>
